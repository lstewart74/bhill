Registration & Fees

Membership & fees information. Become a member and join the BHILL Family.

For over 50 years Beacon Hill Pool has been a staple of summertime outdoor activity in the Beacon Hill Community.

The pool has provided families from throughout Beaconsfield and surrounding neighborhoods with a safe, fun, and friendly locale to escape the summer heat. Come for a swim or just hang with your friends. Stop by and check it out for yourself, you won't be disappointed!

Pool membership entitles the holder access to ALL swim programs except for Bronze Medallion/Cross. This includes open swim times and adult lessons for mom and dad, and lessons and team activities for the kids and teens. Other activities such as kids movie nights and adult parties may involve a small fee for snacks and such.

If you are interested in becoming a member for the 2022 season, please see our fees and registration information below.

Bronze Course Fees

Bronze Medallion

$60

Bronze Medallion teaches the basic lifesaving principles of judgment, knowledge, skill, and physical fitness. Candidates learn rescue procedures for both conscious and unconscious victims. This certificate is required to take any other Lifesaving or Lifeguarding courses. Prerequisites: 13 yrs of age by time of Exam (or Bronze Star) and Gold Swim level (or equivalent).

Bronze Cross

$60

Bronze Cross builds on the lifesaving procedures of Medallion and prepares candidates to assess dangerous areas and dangerous behaviours in order to keep aquatic facilities safe and to prevent accidents. This certificate allows candidates to work as “assistant lifeguards” and is required to take the National Lifeguard course. Prerequisite: Bronze Medallion.

Step 2: Payment

PAYPAL (COMING SOON)

Pay securely with your credit card using PayPal.

Please note that you must complete an application form regardless of your chosen payment method and that your registration information and payment method must match. For Paypal payment select the appropriate option from the menu. You can select more than one option from this list in the event that you are paying for both membership and courses. After your first selection a new browser window will open displaying your PayPal cart. To add another item from the fees list navigate back to the menu and make your second selection. It will automatically be added to your cart.

INTERAC E-TRANSFER

Members also have the option of paying membership fees via interac e-transfer. In the message section of the transfer please enter the membership household last name, then specify what fee(s) the payment is for (refer to the adjacent dropdown menu for list of fees).

Please send all payments to the following recipient email address: bhpooltreasurer@gmail.com. You may choose any security password you like as the account is set up for automatic deposit.

CHEQUE

If you are only able to pay by cheque please contact us to make arrangements. Cheques to be made payable to “Beacon Hill Swimming Pool Club” (NSF fee $25).
